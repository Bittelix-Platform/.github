# Bittelix Platform · AI-Powered Crypto & Forex Trading Ecosystem

**Bittelix** is a unified platform for automated and intelligent trading.  
It combines a strategy marketplace, AI‑driven portfolio management, P2P exchange, and institutional‑grade security — all in one place.

## 🚀 What we offer

- **Strategy Marketplace** – Buy/sell verified trading strategies with Claude Opus 4.7 pre‑filtering
- **AI Smart Portfolio** – Adaptive, self‑optimising autopilot for Crypto & Forex
- **News Guard** – Automatic trading halt during high‑impact news and low‑liquidity hours
- **P2P Marketplace** – Direct peer‑to‑peer crypto trades with fixed fees
- **HD Wallet** – Non‑custodial multi‑chain wallet (10+ assets, unique deposit addresses)
- **Forecast Game** – Prediction market for prices, macro events and more
- **Trader Dashboard** – Standalone management app for professional strategy sellers

## 🧠 Powered by

- Frontend: Next.js (TypeScript)
- Auth & real‑time: Supabase
- High‑load data: Neon (PostgreSQL)
- AI reasoning: Claude Opus 4.7 / 4.5 (Anthropic)
- Charts: TradingView
- Hosting: EU / UAE‑based infrastructure

## 🌍 Core principles

- **Fair access** – One pricing model for everyone, no rigid all‑in‑one plans
- **Security by design** – 3‑layer 2FA, admin‑confirmed large withdrawals, session tracking
- **Transparency** – Live logs, drawdown indicators, risk per trade settings
- **Global ready** – 20 languages, multi‑timezone, support for crypto & forex
